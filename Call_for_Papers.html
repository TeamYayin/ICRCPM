International Conference on Robotics and Cyber Physical Machines

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology(A),Hyderabad, March 24th & 25th, 2023

 

ICRCPM focuses on both theory and applications of Robotics and Cyber Physical Systems.This conference aims to bring together academia, researchers, developers, and practitioners from academia, scientific organizations, and industry to share and disseminate recent research findings in the fields of Computer science,Electronics and Electrical engineering and their applications. ICRCPM is an outstanding platform to discuss the key findings, exchanging novel ideas, listening to world-class leaders, and sharing experiences with peer groups. The conference will provide opportunities for collaboration with the national and international organizations of repute to the research community. ICRCPM expects a large number of participants and submissions from worldwide.

ICRCPM invites the original research papers on the following topics or tracks listed below, but not limited to:

Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS)

  • Cyber physical systems architecture
  • Medical Cyber Physical Systems and Health
  • Networks and Communication in CPS
  • Threats, Security and Privacy for Cyber Physical System
  • Technologies and Applications of Cyber Physical Systems

Robotics

  • Robot Dynamic and Control
  • Human-robot Systems
  • Intelligent Robots
  • Home Robots
  • Man-Machine Systems
  • Human Factors in system Design
  • batteries for Robots.

IOT and Applications

  • Applied IoT
  • Industrial IOT
  • IoT Innovations and Solutions
  • Convergent Technologies

Assistive Technologies

  • Assistive Software Technology
  • Assistive Medical Technology
  • Learning Tools & Education
  • Nanoelectronics and Chip design
  • Embedded System
  • Cognitive Science
